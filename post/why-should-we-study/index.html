Why should we study？

2022-03-27 1 min read # 个人作品 # Gridea

Without the knowledge we can appreciate only the formal content of this world, and we will fail to understand why it was produced or the concepts that shaped life and caused life to adopt its distinctive forms. And remember that, knowledge will accrue to you from reading.

We can't stop for struggling. Meditate on our whole life. Do we really want to settle for pretty good when we can do something very good?

We're imperfect, and we're wired for struggle, but we are worthy for love and belonging.

Put a gallant fight against hard, fully embrace vulnerability, and believe that what made us vulnerable made us beautiful. So don't be afraid of failure,just do it.
